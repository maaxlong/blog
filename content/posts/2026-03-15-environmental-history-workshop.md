Together with Andrew Seaton at the University of Manchester, I am organising a two-day workshop on *Modern British History and the 'Environmental Turn'*. This will take place at Lincoln College, Oxford, on 16-17 September 2026.

Attendees are asked to submit a 300-word proposal for a short (c. 2500 word) 'position paper' to andrew.seaton@manchester.ac.uk by 15 May 2026. Further details in the CfP below.
